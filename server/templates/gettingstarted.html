Getting started

1. Sign in

Upload is only for people who are willing to sign in. See upper right-hand corner.

2. Click 'Upload'

On this page you choose which way to upload.

  • Upload by URL

    The simplest option if all you have is a direct URL to an image.

  • Upload with Filepicker.io™

    Filepicker.io is an external service that makes it really convenient for you to upload using other means such as taking pictures directly from your Dropbox, Box or Google Drive account.

3. Look at your picture

Right after you upload is finished, you can use it.

The first time you load it it's going to pop up a window asking you to enter a title and description for your picture.

4. Annotate your picture

On the left hand side you have tools to draw annotations on the picture.

The annotations you can draw are Line, Polygon, Rectangle, Circle and Marker. All of them plus the title you enter will be available for all to see.

5. Share your picture

Click the "Permalink" link in the upper right-hand corner to get a direct link to the exactly what you see.

You probably have Facebook and Twitter and I'm not going to show you have to use them :)
